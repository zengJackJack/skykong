关于我们

专注板材加工 智能化

Xingkong Technology specializes in intelligent panel processing solutions for the furniture manufacturing industry. Our core products — CutControl panel saw control system and Optimizer sheet nesting software — serve 500+ clients worldwide across China, Southeast Asia, and Europe.
